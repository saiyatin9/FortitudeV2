Woven Gloves

Woven Gloves are robust hand protection garments for heavy industrial tasks. Available in multiple material configurations to match different hazard profiles - abrasion, cut, heat, or chemical protection.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Woven Gloves are offered in three primary variants. The cotton canvas variant provides 360° hand coverage with double-palm stitching for abrasion resistance in general industrial use. The leather-palm variant uses genuine split leather for the palm with a woven cotton back, combining durability with dexterity for precision handling tasks. The FR variant uses inherently flame-resistant Nomex® woven fabric for environments with heat, spark, and flame exposure. All variants are available in knitted or gauntlet cuff options to prevent debris ingress.
