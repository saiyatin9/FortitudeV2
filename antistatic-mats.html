Antistatic Mats

Antistatic Mats are workstation-level ESD control solutions for workbenches, assembly tables, and floor placement. They provide a static-dissipative surface that protects sensitive components and serves as a reliable grounding point for the entire ESD workstation setup.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Antistatic Mats feature a 3-layer construction - a static-dissipative top layer for component safety, a conductive core for reliable charge transfer, and an insulating bottom to prevent unintended discharge paths. Each mat is supplied with a grounding cord and snap connector for simple earthing to the facility ground. The chemical-resistant surface withstands common solvents including IPA, acetone, and mild detergents used in electronics cleaning operations. The non-slip embossed bottom prevents mat movement on smooth workbench surfaces during operation.
