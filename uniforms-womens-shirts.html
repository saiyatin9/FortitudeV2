Women's Shirts

Women's uniform shirts are designed for a professional, tailored fit - combining wrinkle resistance with all-day comfort for customer-facing and administrative roles.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Women's Shirts are produced from a 60/40 Cotton-Polyester poplin or Oxford weave at 110 - 130 GSM, delivering a crisp professional appearance that remains wrinkle-free across full-length shifts. The semi-fitted cut follows the female form with darts for a tailored silhouette, while stretch-panel options maintain freedom of movement for active roles. A concealed button placket is available for a clean front finish in formal environments. Custom logo embroidery, name badges, and epaulettes are accommodated to support corporate identity programmes.
