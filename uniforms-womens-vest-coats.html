Women's Vest Coats

Women's vest coats are tailored outer layering garments available in formal waistcoat and utility vest styles - designed to complement women's uniform programmes with a polished, functional outer layer.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Women's Vest Coats are offered in a formal fitted waistcoat with a princess-seam construction for a tailored silhouette, and a utility vest variant with a full front zip and patch pockets. The formal waistcoat is fully lined in polyester taffeta with a V-neckline and adjustable back strap. The utility vest provides a practical, contemporary look for hospitality and operational roles. Both styles include a branding panel for logo embroidery or heat-transfer print application.
