Women's Churidars

Women's Churidars are tailored in a traditional churidar cut using premium stretch-knit fabric blends - combining cultural authenticity with the comfort and durability demanded by professional workwear programmes.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Women's Churidars are manufactured from a premium 80% Polyester / 15% Viscose / 5% Elastane stretch-knit blend that delivers the characteristic tapered, gathered-at-ankle silhouette of the churidar while maintaining all-day wearing comfort in demanding professional settings. The elasticated waistband allows a secure yet comfortable fit without restricting movement during extended shifts. The high-colour-retention fabric maintains professional colourways across 60+ wash cycles at 40°C. Available in standard and custom sizes for enterprise uniform programmes. Custom colour matching is supported for branded fleet orders.
