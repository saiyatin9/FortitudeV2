Non-Woven Head Caps

Non-Woven Head Caps are lightweight, single-use head coverings that prevent hair and scalp contamination in sterile and food-grade environments. Their breathable construction allows continuous wear through long shifts.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

At just 17 - 25 GSM, Fortitude Fabrics Non-Woven Head Caps impose minimal wearer fatigue even during extended shifts. Manufactured from Spunbond Polypropylene, the breathable pore structure delivers reliable barrier performance while maintaining air circulation. Available in bouffant (round cap), surgeon's cap, and beard net styles to cover all cleanroom and surgical theatre requirements. Individual and bulk pack options are offered, including dispenser box format for cleanroom anteroom use. Latex-free and lint-free construction ensures no secondary contamination risk.
