Antistatic Head Caps

Antistatic Head Caps provide essential scalp and hair coverage in ESD-controlled environments. They prevent loose hair contamination while safely channelling static generated by hair movement away from sensitive components and equipment.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Constructed from a 98% Polyester / 2% Carbon Conductive Fibre blend, Fortitude Fabrics Antistatic Head Caps feature a conductive grid woven evenly across the full cap surface for uniform static dissipation. Flat-stitched seams eliminate contamination trap points, making these caps suitable for both ESD-controlled and hygiene-sensitive environments. The elastic back band provides a comfortable, adjustable fit for extended wear across all head sizes. Available in white, blue, and yellow for colour-coded zoning.
