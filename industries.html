Industries We Serve

From semiconductor fabs to pharmaceutical cleanrooms - our technical textiles meet the exact compliance requirements of the world's most demanding industries.

memory

Semiconductor Manufacturing

ESD-controlled garments certified to IEC 61340-5-1 and ANSI/ESD S20.20 for wafer fab and chip assembly environments. Our antistatic coveralls, caps, shoe covers, and mats form complete ESD workstation solutions.

vaccines

Pharmaceutical

ISO 14644-compliant cleanroom garments for pharmaceutical manufacturing, API handling, and sterile filling. Non-woven and antistatic coveralls available with sterile peel-open packaging for Grade A/B zones.

devices

Electronics & PCB Assembly

Antistatic garments, mats, and gloves for SMT lines, PCB rework stations, and sensitive component storage areas. Validated for continuous ESD protection through rigorous wash-cycle testing.

medical_services

Healthcare

Surgical head caps, non-woven shoe covers, and disposable garments for hospital OTs and wards. EN 13795 and EN 14683 certified. Latex-free construction for allergy-sensitive clinical environments.

directions_car

Automotive

FR coveralls and heavy-duty woven workwear for assembly-line workers, welders, and paint-shop operators. EN ISO 11612 flame protection and ATEX-compliant antistatic garments for explosive environments.

restaurant

Food Processing

Non-woven and woven head caps, shoe covers, and disposable gloves meeting FDA 21 CFR and EU food contact regulations. Colour-coded options for allergen-zone management and HACCP compliance.

science

Laboratories

Chemical-resistant woven coveralls, lint-free cloths, and antistatic gloves for research, analytical, and quality-control laboratories. Compatible with common solvents including IPA, acetone, and acids.

factory

General Manufacturing

Complete workwear programmes - shirts, trousers, vest coats, and coveralls - for manufacturing staff. Custom branding, embroidery, and graded sizing programmes available for enterprise fleet accounts.
