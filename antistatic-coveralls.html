Antistatic Coveralls

The Antistatic Coveralls are full-body protective garments designed for workers operating in electrostatic discharge (ESD) sensitive zones. The fabric incorporates a precisely engineered conductive carbon fibre grid that continuously dissipates static build-up, protecting both personnel and sensitive equipment.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Antistatic Coveralls combine a 65% Polyester / 35% Cotton base fabric with a precision carbon fibre conductive grid to deliver full-body ESD protection compliant with EN 1149-5 and IEC 61340-5-1. The concealed front zip with antistatic flap prevents accidental spark generation, while knitted ESD cuffs ensure a continuous grounding path at the wrists. The breathable fabric structure supports extended wear in climate-controlled cleanroom environments. Available in sizes XS through 5XL with custom sizing and embroidery options, these coveralls are machine washable to 60°C and maintain their antistatic performance for 50+ industrial wash cycles.
