Antistatic Shoe Covers

Antistatic Shoe Covers form the critical ground path in an ESD protective system. They enable safe charge transfer from the wearer to grounded flooring in ESD-protected areas, completing the protective circuit.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Antistatic Shoe Covers use conductive carbon threads running the full length of the sole to maintain a reliable charge dissipation path. The skid-resistant textured sole ensures safe movement on the smooth flooring typical of ESD-protected zones. Available in both disposable and reusable formats - the reusable version features a double-stitched reinforced sole for extended service life. Universal sizing with an elasticated opening allows quick donning over all standard and safety footwear. Certified to maintain conductivity after 40 hours of continuous use per ANSI/ESD S20.20.
