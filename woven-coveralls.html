Woven Coveralls

Woven Coveralls are the backbone of industrial PPE, combining structural fabric integrity with practical design. Engineered to resist abrasion, tears, and light chemical splashes while maintaining all-day comfort in demanding industrial environments.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Woven Coveralls are available in 100% Cotton FR Twill for flame-resistant applications and a Cotton-Polyester Ripstop blend for tear resistance in mechanical environments. At 260 - 320 GSM, these coveralls provide substantial protection without sacrificing wearability. Reinforced double stitching at all high-stress seam points - knees, elbows, and crotch - ensures durability under extreme physical activity. Multiple utility pockets (2 chest, 2 side hip, 1 back, 1 ruler pocket) are included as standard. Available in arc flash, FR, high-visibility, and standard variants across sizes XS - 6XL.
