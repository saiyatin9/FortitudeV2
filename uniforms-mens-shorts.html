Men's Shorts

Men's uniform shorts are designed for warmer climates or active roles requiring lower body freedom of movement while maintaining a professional appearance.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Men's Shorts are cut from a 65/35 Polyester-Cotton twill or 100% Cotton canvas at 180 - 220 GSM, providing a structured professional silhouette suitable for customer-facing roles. A structured waistband with belt loops and inside adjustable tab accommodates varying fit preferences. Available in standard and cargo variants - the cargo option includes two additional side utility pockets with flap closures for operational roles. Pre-shrunk fabric maintains colour integrity after 50+ wash cycles.
