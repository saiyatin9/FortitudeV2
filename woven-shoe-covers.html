Woven Shoe Covers

Woven Shoe Covers provide a reusable, robust solution for footwear protection in environments with chemical exposure risk or contamination-sensitive controlled access areas.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Woven Shoe Covers combine an Oxford weave polyester outer for water and light chemical splash resistance with a cotton twill inner for wearer comfort. Reinforced toe and heel zones significantly extend wear longevity in high-traffic environments. A non-slip rubberised sole patch ensures traction on wet or oily industrial flooring. The elasticated opening accommodates most industrial footwear and safety boots. Available in standard, chemical-splash resistant, and FR flame-retardant variants.
