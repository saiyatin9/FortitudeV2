Certifications & Quality Assurance

Every garment leaving our Hyderabad facility undergoes multi-stage quality control validated against internationally recognised standards. Our certifications are independently verified and continuously maintained.

Our Certification Portfolio

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management System

Our quality management system is certified to ISO 9001:2015, encompassing the full production lifecycle from raw material sourcing through final inspection. Certification is maintained through annual third-party surveillance audits.

OEKO-TEX® Standard 100

Harmful Substance Testing

Selected fabric lines carry the OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 certification, confirming they have been tested for over 100 harmful substances and are safe for prolonged skin contact in all wear categories.

ANSI/ESD S20.20

ESD Control Programme

Our antistatic product line is validated against the ANSI/ESD S20.20 programme standard for protection of electrical and electronic parts, assemblies, and equipment sensitive to electrostatic discharge.

EN 1149

Electrostatic Properties

EN 1149-1, EN 1149-3, and EN 1149-5 certifications cover surface resistivity, charge decay, and performance requirements for electrostatic protective garments used in explosive environments (ATEX).

SGS Audited

Third-Party Factory Audit

SGS facility audits are conducted annually, covering social compliance, manufacturing process integrity, equipment calibration records, and product sampling. Audit reports are available to qualified buyers on request.

CE / EN Standards

European Conformity

Non-woven protective coveralls carry CE marking to EN 13982-1 (Type 5) and EN 13034 (Type 6). Healthcare garments comply with EN 13795 for surgical use. Full Declaration of Conformity documents available.

Our Quality Control Process

science

Incoming Material Inspection

All raw materials are tested on arrival - tensile strength, resistivity, GSM, shrinkage, and colorfastness before entering production.

precision_manufacturing

In-Process Monitoring

Statistical sampling at each production stage. ESD fabric is tested at cutting, sewing, and finishing for consistent resistivity values.

verified

Final Inspection & Certification

Finished goods are batch-tested against applicable standards. Lot traceability records and test certificates accompany each shipment.

Request Certification Documents

Test certificates, Declaration of Conformity documents, and SGS audit reports are available to qualified procurement teams on request.
