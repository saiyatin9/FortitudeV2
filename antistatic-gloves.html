Antistatic Gloves

Antistatic Gloves offer precision hand protection combining ESD safety with tactile sensitivity - ideal for delicate component handling where fine motor control is as important as electrostatic discharge protection.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

The Fortitude Fabrics Antistatic Gloves use a seamless knit Polyester/Carbon fibre construction to deliver homogeneous charge dissipation across the entire hand surface. PU-coated fingertips provide exceptional grip and component handling precision for delicate assembly tasks. The latex-free construction eliminates allergy risk for sensitive workers, and the seamless knit minimises particle generation in cleanroom environments. These gloves withstand 100+ wash cycles without any measurable degradation of antistatic performance, making them a cost-effective choice for ongoing operations.
