Non-Woven Coveralls

Non-Woven Coveralls are single-use or limited-use total-body garments offering barrier protection against particulates, aerosols, light chemical splashes, and biological hazards - with lightweight construction that ensures freedom of movement.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Non-Woven Coveralls are manufactured in SMS (Spunbond-Meltblown-Spunbond) Polypropylene, providing a triple-layer barrier of strength, filtration, and strength. The microporous film-laminated variant offers a water column resistance of 40+ cm for higher liquid hazard environments. Available with sealed, taped, or bound seams depending on the hazard classification required. Elastic wrists, ankles, and integrated hoods provide a full body seal. Sterile variants are available in peel-open pouches for pharmaceutical Grade A/B environments.
