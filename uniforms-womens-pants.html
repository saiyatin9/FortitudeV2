Women's Pants

Women's uniform trousers are cut for a professional appearance with ergonomic comfort - available in both structured formal cuts and practical stretch-blend options for active roles.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Women's Pants are available in a flat-front, semi-fitted cut and an elasticated waistband variant for maximum comfort in dynamic roles. The 220 - 260 GSM fabric delivers structured drape that maintains a polished line throughout extended wear. Two front slash pockets and one rear welt pocket are standard on the formal cut. The stretch-blend variant incorporates 5% elastane for freedom of movement in hospitality, healthcare, and field roles. A stain-release finish is optionally applied for food service and clinical applications. Available in short, regular, and long leg lengths.
