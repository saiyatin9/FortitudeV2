Woven Head Caps

Woven Head Caps provide durable, reusable head coverage for industrial and food processing environments. The tight poplin weave prevents fibre shedding and particle contamination while offering superior comfort during long work shifts.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Constructed from a 65/35 Polyester-Cotton woven poplin at 120 - 140 GSM, Fortitude Fabrics Woven Head Caps offer a reusable, washable alternative to disposable head covers for environments where long-term cost efficiency matters. The structured brim provides light protection in outdoor settings and construction environments. An adjustable Velcro or snap-back closure allows a universal fit for all head sizes. Colourfast for 80+ wash cycles at 60°C - available in plain and high-visibility colourways for site safety zoning.
