Non-Woven Shoe Covers

Non-Woven Shoe Covers are single-use foot covers that prevent contamination carry-in and carry-out between controlled and general areas. Their PE-coated non-slip sole ensures safe use on smooth flooring.

send Send Enquiry

Description

expand_more

Fortitude Fabrics Non-Woven Shoe Covers use a bonded PE-coated sole for water resistance and improved traction on the smooth, tiled surfaces typical of cleanrooms, hospitals, and food production facilities. The elasticated opening accommodates most standard footwear and is available in over-boot sizes for industrial safety footwear. Bulk-packed in multiples of 100 pairs for efficient dispensing. Blue and white colour options support visual management systems for incoming and outgoing contamination zones. Anti-skid sole texture reduces slip risk significantly compared to plain film covers.
